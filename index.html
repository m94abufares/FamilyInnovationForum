أهلاً وسهلاً بكم في ملتقى الابتكار الأسري، حيث نجتمع اليوم للاحتفاء بالإبداع والتفكير الجديد الذي ينبع من قلب الأسرة. إن هذا الملتقى يمثل منصة فريدة لتبادل الأفكار والمشاريع التي تعزز من دور الأسرة كمحور أساسي في الابتكار والتنمية.

إن مفهوم الابتكار الأسري يتجاوز مجرد تطوير المنتجات أو الخدمات؛ فهو يشمل أيضاً كيفية تحسين العلاقات الأسرية وتعزيز التواصل بين أفراد الأسرة. في عالمنا اليوم، حيث تتغير الظروف الاجتماعية والاقتصادية بسرعة، تصبح الأسر بحاجة إلى أدوات وأساليب جديدة لمواجهة التحديات. وهنا يأتي دور منهجية ستيم ( (STEMالتي تجمع بين العلوم، والتكنولوجيا، والهندسة، والفنون، والرياضيات، لتوفير إطار عمل شامل يمكن الأسر من استكشاف الأفكار الجديدة وتطبيقها.

رئيس مجلس الإدارة

the Director of the Training and Development Department

In today’s rapidly evolving world, the concept of family extends beyond traditional definitions. Families come in many forms, and each unique structure brings its own strengths and perspectives. It is within these diverse family units that we often find the seeds of creativity and innovation. Families are where we learn to collaborate, to communicate, and to solve problems together. They are our first support systems, our first teams, and our first laboratories for experimentation.

Innovation is not just about technology or business; it is about finding new ways to connect, to support one another, and to improve our lives. At the heart of every innovative idea is a need—a need that often arises from our experiences within our families. Whether it’s developing new educational tools for our children, creating healthier meal options for our loved ones, or finding ways to balance work and home life, family drives our desire to innovate.

  1. About KKIS Family Innovation Forum

    The Family Innovation Forum is typically an event that focuses on fostering innovation and collaboration among students with their families, organizations, communities within KKIS.

    it often aims to address STEAM challenges faced by our students, promote best practices, and share resources that can help improve dynamics and well-being.

General objective

Deepening the impact of the STEAM methodology on students and their families by creating innovation projects, focusing on fostering creativity, collaboration, and practical solutions in these interconnected fields

Forum Vision

The vision of a Family Innovation Forum typically revolves around creating a supportive and collaborative environment that empowers families to thrive in an ever-changing world
